Shot in a continuous take, the film walks through a vintage house, unfolding the nature of family history and the influences that either can destroy us or help us grow. With each room the scenes gradually become more intense and traumatic.

Family influence can become overpowering to the point that we lose our sense of self. But by confronting these influences rather than avoiding them, one can reconcile their past with their present, and use family history as a foundation for growth and freedom.
