Masha Cherezova is a choreographer and dancer based in LA. Her work has been performed at local esteemed venues including L.A. Dance Project, the Carpenter Performing Arts Center, and USC School of Dance. Masha’s work extends to film, notably choreographing Laufey’s Silver Lining Music video with 10 dancers, and movement directing for DJI at the 2025 NAB show. In 2024, she collaborated with Onco-Ballet Foundation and cancer survivors to create Flock, which exhibited at the SoCal Ballet 2024 Festival. In 2023 and 2025 Masha was the resident choreographer at V&T Classical Ballet Academy, coreographing Lemons? and Spring, two surrealistic evening-length works.

Masha’s professional ballet career was based in Russia at the Novosibirsk Theatre of Opera and Ballet, sharing the stage with ballet stars Natalia Osipova, Denis Rodkin, Evgenia Obraztsova; touring in China, Russia, France, and the Netherlands.
